The sky above the Scorched Desert was a bruised, bleeding purple. Arin, the young Sand Mage, stood at the precipice of the Dune of Echoes, his staff planted firmly in the shifting sands. Wind howled past him, carrying the distant roar of a Sand Wyrm. He squinted into the storm.
"They're coming," he whispered to himself, a grim realization settling in his gut.
Suddenly, the sand erupted. A massive serpentine beast burst forth, scales glinting like obsidian. Arin raised his staff, a blinding white light gathering at the tip.
"Not today!" he shouted, unleashing a shockwave of energy that collided with the beast. The impact sent a shockwave across the desert. He gritted his teeth, the effort draining his mana. But he couldn't stop. He was the last line of defense.
